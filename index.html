Planet Fitness isn't Really that Cheap

When you sit down at the end of a day with a, possibly unhealthy snack in your lap, it's inevitable that you will see a commercial for a gym membership or an at home DVD set. The main one I always see, is the Planet Fitness commercial. This commercial has everyone believing that Planet Fitness is the cheap and non-judgmental option that makes it easy for you to get off your butt and get in shape. I am here to tell you, it's not that cheap and it's not your best option.
